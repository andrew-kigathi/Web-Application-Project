Frequently Asked Questions

Are the melody loops and sounds royalty-free?

Yes! All individual melody loops available for download on AK Sounds are 100% royalty-free. You can use them in your beats, commercial releases, beat leases, and performances without owing any royalties or giving split percentages.

How do I download a sound?

Simply hover over or tap the sound card you want, and click the download icon (⬇). The audio file will automatically save to your device. No complicated checkout process is required for free loops.

What audio format do the downloads come in?

Currently, the individual loops on our site are provided in high-quality, 320kbps MP3 format for fast browsing and downloading. High-resolution WAV formats are included in our premium downloadable Kits.

Do I need to create an account to use the site?

You can listen to and download the free loops without an account. However, creating a free account allows you to save your favorite sounds, organize your library, and get exclusive access to premium Kits before they drop to the public.

How often is the sound library updated?

We update our library weekly. We frequently add new melodies to all branches, including Rap, RnB, Guitar, and Ethereal sections, so be sure to check back often for fresh inspiration.
